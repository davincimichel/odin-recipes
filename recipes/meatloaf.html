Meatloaf Recipe

The Perfect Meatloaf

Nothing fills my heart more than a nice, homemade meatloaf. This one in particular is sure to get your mouth glands salivating

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Tbs flour
  • 1/3 cup of ketchup
  • 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs
  • paprika

Steps

  1. mash up da meat
  2. crack da eggs
  3. mix it all together
  4. see what comes out
